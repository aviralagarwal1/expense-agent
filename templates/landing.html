Workflow
A short path from screenshots to structure

The product should feel closer to a focused review desk than a bulky finance dashboard. Each step is deliberately narrow so the important decisions stay obvious.

01

Drop in screenshots

Upload as many images as you need, even when there is overlap across card views or days.

02

Let the app classify

Claude extracts expenses, the backend normalizes merchants, and likely duplicates get separated.

03

Confirm the final list

Add the new transactions, inspect edge cases, and keep a monthly history ready for export later.
