Hi! I'm Aviral, a Business Honors and Information Systems student at UT Austin.

I created Expense Agent as a personal project to track expenses across different credit card apps in one unified log. The main challenge was building the application to extract data from screenshots, cross-check entries against user history, filter duplicates, and summarize spending.

Through this, I became more interested in designing intuitive user flows and thinking from the customer's perspective. AI has made it easier than ever to build, but I'm focused on developing stronger product judgment. I want to better understand which features reduce friction, what to prioritize, and when simplicity matters more than adding functionality.

If you have feedback or ideas, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, or X. If you're interested in following my work, visit aviralagarwal.com.
