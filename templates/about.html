Hi! I'm Aviral, a Business Honors and Information Systems student at UT Austin.

I built Compline as a personal project to track expenses across multiple credit card apps in a single unified log. The core challenge was extracting structured data from screenshots, deduplicating against user history, and presenting insights in a simple, intuitive way.

Through this project, I became deeply interested in product design and user behavior. While AI has lowered the barrier to building, I’m focused on developing stronger product judgment: how to reduce friction, what to prioritize, and when simplicity matters more than added functionality.

If you have feedback or ideas, feel free to reach out via LinkedIn, GitHub, X, or email. You can also follow my work at aviralagarwal.com.
